About this Archive Class A — District II — Authorized
Archive Registry — District II

Dustarchive is a documentation project focused on monumental earthworks — structures that exist in relationship with geological time rather than human time.

We record works that resist the gallery, the museum, the white cube. Works that require travel, endurance, and encounter with scale that cannot be reproduced.

The desert is not a backdrop. It is a medium. Dust is not absence — it is accumulation.
